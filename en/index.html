Biography

Lingxiao Zhang is a master of artificial intelligence at the Intelligent Computing Lab, Tsinghua University. His research interests include natural language processing, recommendation system, computer vision and machine learning. He has worked in Alibaba Group, Google, Minute Data Information Technology.
